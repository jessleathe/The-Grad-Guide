What Is This?

Think of this of as your insider playbook for breaking into tech.

Landing a tech job is hard until someone shows you exactly how it’s done. This guide covers every stage, every decision, and every tool you need to go from applicant to offer.

It contains a total of eight chapters, built by people who’ve been exactly where you are.

Built for you if:

✔ You’re a college senior or recent graduate

✔ You want a tech job but don’t know where to start

✔ You’re done with vague advice and want actual steps
